My Story

I'm a real estate agent born and raised in the Antelope Valley, bilingual in English and Spanish, and I serve both communities the same way: with honesty first. I guide you toward the best outcome for what you're trying to do — not whatever sounds good in the moment. I treat my clients like family because most of them started as exactly that — friends, neighbors, people who trusted me with one of the biggest decisions of their lives. That trust is the whole job, and when you work with me, I'm committed to you from the first conversation all the way past the closing table. The deals people remember aren't the easy ones. They're the ones where something almost fell apart — a lender mistake, a number that didn't add up, a closing that nearly cost a client money they shouldn't have paid. Those are the moments I show up hardest. When it matters most, that's when I go to work for you. Whether you're buying your first home, selling one you've outgrown, or just trying to figure out what your property's worth — I'll work as hard for you as I would for my own family.
